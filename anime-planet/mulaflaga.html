Welcome friends,

to my anime corner on the web. I started watching anime on Italian TV channels as a kid in the early 1980s, and I am primarily interested in sci-fi and fantasy anime. Still, I do try anything that catches my eye, irrespective of the genre. I collect a wide range of anime-related items and enjoy creating dioramas for photography purposes. I also like cosplay and reenactment photography. Time flies, and the anime world has undergone drastic changes in quality, quantity, and availability since its early days. You start to get the idea when most of your favorite anime gets rebooted. It's often great to watch an old anime brought back to life with modern animation and ideas, reintroducing a series and sometimes even offering a better plot and conclusion. These days, I keep close track of every season (thanks to A-P) and am constantly on the lookout for cool new stuff. I want my profile to be a nice stop offering ideas on what to watch from the current and past seasons. For this purpose, I create lists highlighting those that are some of the best anime, not to be missed by anyone, not just newbies. As a general rule, I do not consider sequel seasons in my rankings but rather focus on new first seasons to provide exposure to a wider variety of anime. Like most people using A-P, I ensure that I rank all anime I have watched using the standard five-star system (5 being the highest rating).
